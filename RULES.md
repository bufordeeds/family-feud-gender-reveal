# 📋 How Family Feud Works (Party Edition)

The rules below follow the real show's format ([official rules](https://www.ultraboardgames.com/family-feud/game-rules.php),
[Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Family_Feud)), adapted for a
living-room party. The app keeps score — you (the host) run the show.

## The big picture

- Two teams compete to guess the answers to survey questions
  ("We asked 100 people…").
- Answers are hidden on the board, ranked by popularity — **#1 is the most
  popular answer**. More popular = more points.
- Correct guesses add points to the round's **POT**. One team wins the pot
  each round. Highest total score at the end wins the game.

## Round flow

### 1. The Face-Off
One player from each team steps up. The host reads the question, and the
first player to shout (or slap the table — house rules!) answers first.

- If they hit the **#1 answer**, their team takes control immediately.
- Otherwise the other face-off player answers, and whoever gave the
  **higher-ranked answer** wins control for their team.
- Neither answer on the board? Go back and forth until someone hits one.
- *(Show rule: the controlling team may "play or pass" — let the other team
  play the board instead. Optional at a party; most groups just play.)*

> **In the app:** reveal each face-off guess that's on the board as it's
> made (press its number).

### 2. Playing the board
The controlling team goes down their line, **one guess per person, no
huddling**:

- **On the board** → reveal it *(ding!)* — points go into the pot.
- **Not on the board** (or they freeze up — give them ~5 seconds) →
  **STRIKE** ❌ *(buzzer!)*
- **Clear every answer** before striking out → they bank the pot instantly.

> **In the app:** press the answer's number to reveal, `X` for a strike.
> The strike ❌s appear huge on the TV — milk it.

### 3. Three strikes → the Steal
When the controlling team collects **3 strikes ❌❌❌**, the other team
gets ONE chance to steal:

- They **may huddle** and agree on a single answer (captain has final say).
- If it's on the board → **they steal the entire pot!**
- If not → the original team keeps the pot.

> **In the app:** reveal the steal answer if it's on the board, then award
> the pot with `Q` (Team 1) or `W` (Team 2).

### 4. End of round
- Reveal any remaining answers for the fun "OHHH, that was up there!"
  moment (press their numbers).
- Press `→` for the next question — pot and strikes reset automatically.

## Winning

The TV show plays fixed rounds with point multipliers (later rounds count
double and triple) and ends with the famous *Fast Money* bonus round. For a
party, simpler is better:

- Pick your number of rounds up front — **5–7 works great** (the app has 12
  questions loaded).
- Optional drama: announce the last round is **double points** (just award
  the pot to the winning team twice).
- After the final round, hit **🏆 AND THE WINNER IS… 🏆** (or press `R`):
  drumroll, suspense, then confetti in the winning team's colors.

## Host cheat sheet

| Moment | You press |
|--------|-----------|
| Guess is on the board | Its number (`1`–`8`) — check [HOST-KEY.md](HOST-KEY.md) on your phone |

| Round over, award the pot | `Q` (Team 1) or `W` (Team 2) |
| Next question | `→` |
| Game over | `R` — the winner announcement 🏆 |
