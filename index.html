📋 How to Play

1

The Face-Off

One player from each team steps up. First to shout an answer gets to guess. Hit the #1 answer and your team takes control. Miss, and the other player gets a shot at a higher-ranked answer to steal control.

2

Play the Board

The team in control guesses answers one at a time — no huddling. Right answers reveal on the board and add to the pot. Wrong answers earn a strike ❌.

3

Three Strikes → Steal

❌❌❌ and it's the other team's turn — they huddle and get ONE guess. On the board? They steal the whole pot! Miss, and the first team keeps it.

🏆

Winning

Play as many questions as you like. Whoever has the most points at the end wins — hit "And the Winner Is…" for the big finale!
